Test Subject: Substance Composer

Substance Composer is a modern workspace for creating professional web-first publications.

[ Learn more ]

How to test?

Try to create real articles, publish them online and let us know. Tell us what you like about the interface and what you think could be improved. We have an open ear to your feedback and it's very possible that good suggestions will make it into the final product. To get in touch, email us contribute@substance.io. Try to keep the mails short and informal. We'll try to respond as quickly as possible.

Read the user manual

Make sure you read the user manual. We'll keep that document updated to describe new features, known issues and point you to useful resources.

Be aware of known issues

Do not use the OSX Edit menu yet. It is not connected properly. Instead use CMD+C and CMD+V for copy and paste.

Contribute

Substance is being developed since 2010 and has been, for the most part, a voluntary effort. We really need your help now. Please, spread the word about Substance and consider a donation if you like our platform. You can either use Paypal (see link below) or do a bank transfer. Send us an email to contribute@substance.io. We will send you an invoice and provide you with bank details. It is also possible to micro-fund specific features. Talk to us!
